# Assets Directory

Place your logo files here:

## Logo Files Needed:

1. **Think Sync Logo** (for left side of header)
   - Recommended: `thinksync-logo.svg` or `thinksync-logo.png`
   - Preferred format: SVG (scalable) or PNG with transparent background
   - Dimensions: 200x60px or similar aspect ratio

2. **TriColor Initiatives Logo** (for right side of header)
   - Recommended: `tricolor-logo.svg` or `tricolor-logo.png`
   - Preferred format: SVG (scalable) or PNG with transparent background
   - Dimensions: 100x100px or similar (square format)

After adding the logo files, they will be automatically imported in `NavBar.jsx`
